Nostr Extension Helper

This page enables your Nostr extension to access NIP-07 signing capabilities.

Keep this tab open while using the extension's contacts or relays pages.

Checking for NIP-07 provider...
What is this?
Browser extensions cannot directly access window.nostr in their own pages due to security restrictions. This helper page acts as a bridge, allowing your extension to communicate with your NIP-07 signer (like Alby, nos2x, etc.).
